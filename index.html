About

Physicist & Human being.

Bachelor & Master in Physics at Instituto Balseiro, Argentina. I studied ultra high energy cosmic rays at the Pierre Auger Observatory, and some other weird particules in the Photonic & Optomechanics Laboratory, Bariloche Atomic Center.

Currently, I'm a PhD Student in the DESY ATLAS Group in Hamburg, Germany, in collaboration with Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin and Jülich Supercomputing Centre.

I work on Machine Learning approaches to Particle Physics. Also I'm part of the tutorial classes of Introduction to Machine Learning for Physicists in Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin during the winter term 2021-2022. You can find some of the material I made for these clases on your left side.

I play many games, mostly RPG PC games. Active player on League of Legends.
